About Us

Welcome to our educational website designed to help students learn and review lessons from Week 1 to Week 17 in the subjects FILDIS (Filipino sa Iba't Ibang Disiplina), SOSLIT (Sosyedad at Literatura), and KOMFIL (Komunikasyon sa Akademikong Filipino).

This website was developed by BSIT 1st Year students from Section 12021 at Bestlink College of the Philippines as part of our academic project. Our goal is to make learning more accessible and organized by presenting the lessons in a clear, user-friendly format.

Our Mission

To create a centralized and accessible learning platform for Filipino subjects to help students succeed academically.

Our Vision

We envision a community of learners empowered by digital tools and collaboration.
